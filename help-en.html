📖 Getting Started

👨‍👩‍👧
1. Create Parent Account
Open the app, select "Parent", and sign up with your email address.
👧
2. Add a Child
Tap "Add Child" on the dashboard. Set a name, grade, username, and password.
🔑
3. Child Logs In
On the child screen, log in using the username and password you set up.
🚀
4. Start Using
Manage tasks, take learning quizzes, care for your virtual pet, and earn rewards!

💬 Frequently Asked Questions

What age group is KidsToDo for?
KidsToDo is designed for children aged 5 to 12. Learning quizzes cover content from 1st through 6th grade.
How many children can I add?
The free plan supports up to 2 children. Plus and Pro plans allow more.
How are quiz questions selected?
Questions are chosen by an adaptive algorithm based on your child's grade and performance. It increases difficulty for strong areas and provides extra practice for weak ones, using spaced repetition for optimal retention.
How do points and rewards work?
Parents assign points to tasks. When a child completes a task and the parent approves it, points are awarded. Children can exchange accumulated points for rewards from their wish list. Parents set the rewards and required points.
What is the virtual pet?
Each child gets a virtual pet to take care of by feeding, playing, and answering quizzes. The pet grows, levels up, and evolves as the child completes tasks and learns.
Is my child's data safe?
Yes. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell data to third parties and use no ads or tracking SDKs.
Can another parent co-manage?
Yes. Use the "Family Members" feature to invite another parent with an invite code. Both parents can manage tasks, approve submissions, and view reports.
What if I forget my password?
Parent account: Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen to receive a reset email. Child account: Reset the password from the parent's settings screen.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account". All data will be permanently erased. This action cannot be undone.
Does it work offline?
Learning quizzes can work offline with cached questions. However, submitting tasks and syncing new data requires an internet connection.
