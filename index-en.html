Parents guide. Children grow. Together.

📱 iOS 👶 Ages 5-12 🔒 Safe & Private

Features

Everything your family needs to stay organized, learn, and have fun — all in one app.

✅
Task Management
Assign chores, homework & activities. Children submit photo proof, parents approve & award points.
📚
Learning Quizzes
Fun quizzes in literacy & math, matched to grade level. Auto-generated with SVG illustrations.
🐾
Virtual Pet
Complete tasks to grow your pet. Feed, play, quiz — watch it level up and evolve!
🎁
Rewards System
Save up points and trade them for real rewards on the wish list. Builds goal-setting skills.
📖
Picture Diary
Record daily moments in a picture diary. Develop writing skills and self-expression.
📊
Learning Reports
Parent dashboard with real-time progress, accuracy rates, and weekly reports delivered automatically.

How It Works

Three simple steps to get your family started

1
Parent Creates Account
Sign up with email and add your child's profile. That's it.
2
Set Up Tasks & Learning
Customize chores, quizzes, points, and rewards for your family.
3
Kids Have Fun
Children use their own screen to complete tasks, take quizzes, and grow their pet!

Built for Safety

Your child's privacy is our top priority

🔐
COPPA Compliant — Only parents can create child accounts. Minimal data collection.
🛡️
Encrypted — All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS. Passwords stored hashed.
🚫
No Ads — Zero third-party ads or tracking SDKs. We never sell children's data.
🗑️
Full Deletion — Delete account = all data permanently erased. Parents in full control.
