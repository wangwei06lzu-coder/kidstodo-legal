SETUP

Get started in 5 minutes

Set up the system once. After that, your child runs it themselves.

1
Download the app and create a parent account
Free download from the App Store. Pick "Parent," sign up with email. Then enter your child's name and grade — the app generates a kid login for them automatically.
2
Set rewards = screen time
Your kid can request rewards through their Wishlist. You just approve. Adding a few defaults at the start helps things flow.
EXAMPLES • 30 min gaming = 100 pt
• 15 min YouTube = 50 pt
• 1 hour iPad time = 200 pt
• 1 hour playing outside with friends = 80 pt
3
Set up earning tasks
Daily routines, chores, homework, healthy habits — everything that gives points. Kid does it → you approve → points credited.
EXAMPLES • Brush teeth = +10 pt (daily)
• Homework = +30 pt (daily)
• Wash dishes = +15 pt (twice a week)
• Tidy room = +50 pt (weekend)
• 30 min reading = +20 pt (daily)
4
Bonus points from learning — make studying rewarding
About 10,000 in-app quiz questions covering math, reading, science, social studies for elementary grades. 2 pt per correct answer, bonus 10 pt for streaks of 5. When the kid wants 20 more points, they go study — on their own.
LEARNING EARNINGS • 5 correct answers = +10 pt
• 10 in a row = +25 pt (bonus)
• Daily streak grows for additional bonuses
5
Done. Now just watch.
On their own home screen, your kid sees today's tasks, point balance, and "X points to next reward" — and plans their own day.
You just approve when they submit. The "did you do your homework?" reminders disappear.
